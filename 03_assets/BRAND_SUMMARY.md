# Interloom — Brand Summary
> Extracted from Manual de Marca 2026 (by Anita)

---

## Logo

**Icon:** 4-leaf clover with a green-to-yellow gradient (light center → dark green edges)  
**Wordmark:** "INTERLOOM" in Recoleta Regular, near-black (#1D1D1B)

### Versions
| Version | When to use |
|---------|-------------|
| Horizontal (icon + text side by side) | Default — most uses |
| Vertical (icon above text) | Compact/square spaces |
| Isologotipo (icon only) | App icon, favicon, small marks |
| Positive (solid black) | Merchandising only |
| Negative (white on dark) | Merchandising only |

### Size rules
- Horizontal minimum: **3 cm**
- Vertical minimum: **2.3 cm**
- Always maintain clear space equal to the "O" letter around the logo

### Where it CAN go
- White backgrounds ✅
- Light/off-white backgrounds ✅
- Fine, subtle textures (linen, paper) ✅

### Where it CANNOT go
- Strong/saturated colors (blue, red, etc.) ❌
- Dark backgrounds ❌
- Busy/detailed photos ❌

## Brand Design Principles (from Mazor's brief)

1. **Formalidad y profesionalismo** — Serious, organized, high corporate standards. Consistent across all divisions.
2. **Solidez operativa** — Communicates track record, operational capacity, process control — not a conceptual brand.
3. **Cercanía al campo sin clichés** — Natural, honest, contemporary. Avoids agricultural stereotypes.
4. **Sostenibilidad operativa** — Sustainability as a core operating principle, not decoration. Expressed through tone and visual coherence, not obvious symbols.
5. **Claridad y orden** — Clean, well-hierarchized, easy to read. Critical for web and presentations.
6. **Modernidad atemporal** — Modern but timeless. Minimalist with character. Elegant, not cold.
7. **Cool con identidad de campo** — "Hipster field" aesthetic — sober, elegant, authentic. Never caricature or forced.
8. **Sistema de marca** — Logo and assets must work as a flexible system across web, presentations, email signatures, merch, light + dark backgrounds.
